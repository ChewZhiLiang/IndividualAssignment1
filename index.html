My Journey to Becoming a Professional Musician

Music has been my passion since childhood, and my dream is to make it my life's work. As a professional musician, I aim to combine performance, composition, and teaching to create a sustainable career doing what I love.

The music industry offers diverse paths - from live performance to studio work, from film scoring to music education. My focus is on developing strong instrumental skills while building expertise in music production and technology.

This website outlines my roadmap to success, including the steps I need to take and the resources that inspire me along the way. Whether you're on a similar path or just curious about a musician's journey, I hope you find value in these pages.
